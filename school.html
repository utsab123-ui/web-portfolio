Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for admission? expand_more

You can apply online through our 'Apply Now' portal or visit the campus office for a physical application form. Ensure you have the required documents ready.

What is the student-teacher ratio? expand_more

We maintain a healthy 15:1 student-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention and effective learning outcomes for every student.

Are extracurricular activities mandatory? expand_more

While not strictly mandatory for passing, we highly encourage all students to participate in at least one club or sport for holistic development.

Do you provide transport facilities? expand_more

Yes, we have a fleet of air-conditioned, GPS-tracked buses covering all major parts of the city with trained attendants.
